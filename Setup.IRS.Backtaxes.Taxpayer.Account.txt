Setting up an account with the Electronic Federal Tax Payment System (EFTPS) is straightforward and can be done online, over the phone, or through your tax professional, payroll service, or financial institution. Here's how to do it online:

**1. Gather necessary information:**

* **Taxpayer Identification Number (TIN):** This is your Employer Identification Number (EIN) for businesses or your Social Security Number (SSN) for individuals.
* **Bank account details:** You'll need your bank account number, routing number, and account type (checking or savings).
* **Contact information:** This includes your phone number and email address.

**2. Go to the EFTPS website:**

* Visit [https://www.irs.gov/payments/eftps-the-electronic-federal-tax-payment-system](https://www.irs.gov/payments/eftps-the-electronic-federal-tax-payment-system).
* Click on the **Enrollment** tab.

**3. Create an account:**

* Enter your TIN and select your tax type (individual or business).
* Follow the on-screen instructions to provide your personal information, contact information, and bank account details.
* Choose a secure username and password.
* Review and submit your enrollment application.

**4. Verify your enrollment:**

* You'll receive a temporary Internet password (TIP) via email or mail within 5 business days.
* Log in to EFTPS using your TIP and create a permanent password.
* Once you've verified your enrollment, you can start making payments.

**Alternative enrollment methods:**

* **Phone:** Call the EFTPS Customer Service at 1-800-555-4477.
* **Tax professional:** You can request your tax professional to enroll you in EFTPS on your behalf.
* **Payroll service or financial institution:** If you use a payroll service or financial institution for tax payments, they may offer EFTPS enrollment assistance.

**Additional points to note:**

* New enrollments can take up to 5 business days to process.
* EFTPS offers multi-factor authentication (MFA) for added security.
* You can manage your account, make payments, view payment history, and schedule future payments through EFTPS.

Here are some helpful resources:

By following these steps, you can easily set up an EFTPS account and enjoy the convenience and security of paying your federal taxes electronically.
