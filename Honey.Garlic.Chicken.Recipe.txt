Honey Garlic Chicken Recipe

12.5 oz canned chicken breast (drained) or 6 boneless, skinless chicken thighs

¼ cup all-purpose flour (optional)

1 teaspoon ground paprika 

½ teaspoon kosher salt

½ teaspoon ground black pepper 

1 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes 

1/2 teaspoon chili powder

1/2 teaspoon cajun seasoning 

1/2 teaspoon onion salt

1/4-1/2 teaspoon adobo seasoning 

1/4 teaspoon coriander 

4 cloves garlic minced or 1/2 teaspoons garlic powder

¼ cup molasses or honey or agave nectar  2 tablespoons brown sugar (sweet) or low-sodium soy sauce (sour)

1 cap full vanilla extract

1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
