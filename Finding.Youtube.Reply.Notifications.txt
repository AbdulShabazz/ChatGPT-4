Here's a breakdown of where to find notifications for replies to your comments on YouTube and how to adjust your notification settings:

**Where to Find Notifications:**

* **YouTube Inbox:**
    * **Web:** Click on the bell icon in the top right corner of the YouTube website. This will open your notifications inbox.
    * **Mobile App:** Tap on the 'Inbox' tab at the bottom of your screen, then select the 'Notifications' section.
* **Email:** If you have email notifications enabled, you'll receive emails when someone replies to your comment.

**How to Manage Notification Settings:**

**Web (Computer):**

1. Go to YouTube.com and sign in to your account.
2. Click on your profile picture in the top-right corner.
3. Select "Settings".
4. Click on "Notifications" in the left-side menu.
5. Under "Your Preferences", ensure that the box next to "Replies to my comments" is checked to receive notifications.

**Mobile App:**

1. Open the YouTube app.
2. Tap on your profile picture in the top-right corner.
3. Tap on "Settings".
4. Tap on "Notifications". 
5. Make sure "Replies to my comments" is toggled on.

**Email Notifications:**

1. Follow the steps above to access your YouTube notification settings.
2. Under "Email Notifications," turn on "Send me emails about my YouTube activity and updates I requested".

**Important Notes:**

* You may not receive notifications for every single reply due to YouTube's filtering of consecutive comments.
* If you're not seeing notifications even with settings enabled, ensure your device's notification permissions for YouTube are turned on.
