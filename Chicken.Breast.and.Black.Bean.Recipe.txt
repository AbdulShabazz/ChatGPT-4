Here is a recipe for **Cuban-Style Chicken and Black Beans** that you can make with canned chicken strips, molasses, brown sugar, cinnamon, vanilla extract, and black beans and brown rice:

Ingredients:

* 1 tablespoon olive oil
* 1 onion, chopped
* 2 cloves garlic, minced
* 1/2 cup chopped bell pepper
* 1 teaspoon ground cumin
* 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
* 1/4 teaspoon chili powder
* 1/4 teaspoon salt
* 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
* 1 (14.5 ounce) can diced tomatoes, undrained
* 1 (15 ounce) can black beans, drained and rinsed
* 1 (10 ounce) can canned chicken strips, drained
* 1/4 cup molasses
* 1/4 cup packed light brown sugar
* 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
* 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
* 1 cup cooked black beans and brown rice

Instructions:

1. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the onion, garlic, and bell pepper and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
2. Add the cumin, oregano, chili powder, salt, and black pepper and cook for 1 minute more.
3. Stir in the diced tomatoes, black beans, chicken strips, molasses, brown sugar, cinnamon, and vanilla extract. Bring to a simmer and cook for 10 minutes, or until the chicken is heated through.
4. Serve over cooked black beans and brown rice.

This recipe is easy to make and is perfect for a weeknight dinner. The molasses and brown sugar give the chicken a sweet and savory flavor, and the cinnamon and vanilla extract add a hint of warmth. The Cuban-style black beans and brown rice are a delicious and nutritious accompaniment.

Enjoy!
