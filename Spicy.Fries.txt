What a some spicy sauce and seasonings for baked fries?

ChatGPT:

Certainly! Here are some spicy sauces and seasonings you can use to elevate your baked fries:

Spicy Sauces:

    Sriracha Mayo: Mix Sriracha sauce with mayonnaise for a creamy yet spicy dip.
    Chipotle Aioli: Blend canned chipotle peppers in adobo sauce with mayonnaise.
    Spicy Ketchup: Mix ketchup with cayenne pepper or hot sauce of your choice.
    Buffalo Sauce: A combination of melted butter and hot sauce, typically used for wings.
    Harissa Dip: Harissa is a spicy red chili paste that's great when mixed with a bit of yogurt or olive oil.

Spicy Seasonings: (Mix these seasonings with olive oil or melted butter, and toss your fries in them before baking.)

    Cajun Spice Mix: A combination of paprika, cayenne, garlic powder, onion powder, salt, black pepper, and dried oregano.
    Chili Powder Mix: Use chili powder, cumin, garlic powder, and onion powder for a smoky kick.
    Curry Spiced Fries: Turmeric, cumin, coriander, and a pinch of cayenne.
    Jerk Seasoning: This Jamaican blend consists of allspice, thyme, paprika, cayenne, cinnamon, and brown sugar.
    Peri Peri Seasoning: Paprika, garlic powder, onion powder, cayenne, oregano, and a squeeze of lemon juice.

You can experiment with these suggestions and adjust the levels of spiciness according to your preference. Enjoy your spicy baked fries!
